    About Us

    Who We Are

    Sodo Sports Group is a leading sports medicine and rehabilitation organization dedicated to helping athletes at all levels recover from injuries and return to peak performance. Founded with the vision of transforming sports healthcare in Kenya, we work closely with teams, coaches, and medical professionals to ensure optimal recovery outcomes.

    Our Mission

    To provide comprehensive, science-backed recovery programs that help athletes navigate the critical first phase of injury rehabilitation, reducing recovery times and preventing re-injury through personalized care plans and cutting-edge techniques.

    Our Approach

    At Sodo Sports Group, we understand that the first phase of recovery is critical. Our three-pillar approach ensures athletes receive:

    1. Immediate Assessment: Quick and accurate diagnosis of injuries
    2. Personalized Recovery Plans: Tailored to each athlete's specific needs
    3. Continuous Monitoring: Regular progress tracking with adjustments as needed

    We've helped athletes reduce recovery times by up to 30% through our evidence-based methods.
